"where did antonio go?"

story:
you, the protagonist are named roberto and your sadistic brother antonio suddenly went missing one day.
your abusive father told you to go search for him in the forest which is right next to your house.
you enter the forest and get lured into a van by a snake man with a sword named alesandro. you beg to be freed and
to keep your virginity so he shrinks you to the size of an piano key and makes you traverse a series of tubes inside 
his van in order for him to only abuse one of your holes.

gameplay:
you start in the bottom of alesandro's van engine and you have to fight common house geckos -which are alot larger than you- 
to move on. you have to roll a 6-sided dice and get atleast a 5 to kill a gecko
